The El Dorado Fire

In September 2020, a smoke-generating pyrotechnic device used to reveal a baby’s gender ignited the four-foot tall grass at El Dorado Ranch Park, causing a wildfire that covered more than twenty thousand acres in Southern California, taking the life of a firefighter and forcing many residents to evacuate.

† Morales, Christina and Allyson Waller. “A Gender-Reveal Celebration Is Blamed for a Wildfire…” The New York Times, Sept. 7, 2020.
